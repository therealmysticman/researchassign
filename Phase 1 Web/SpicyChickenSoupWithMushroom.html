Spicy Chicken Soup with Mushrooms

Description

A healthy, nutritious soup is suitable for all ages. It has many benefits and does not cause obesity.

Recipe

  • Tom Yum soup consists of galangal, lemongrass, kaffir lime leaves.
  • shallots (peeled) 4-5 heads
  • Bird's Eye Chilli
  • Straw Mushroom
  • Grape Tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon wet tamarind
  • 1+1/2 tbsp fish sauce
  • lemon
  • thinly sliced chicken breast
  • parsley

Method

  1. boil water
  2. Put the Tom Yum ingredients, the galangal is pounded before putting it in. Do not add too much. Smash the lemongrass and cut it into small pieces, put it in until fragrant. Followed by shallots and tomatoes.
  3. when everything falls into place, add the sliced chicken breast, wait until cooked, then add the mushrooms.
  4. Season with tamarind juice and fish sauce. Remove the pot from the stove.
  5. Then tear kaffir lime leaves and sprinkle them on top. Add crushed bird's eye chili to increase the spiciness. and sprinkle parsley and add seasoning to your liking
