Spicy Mixed Vegetable Soup

Description

Mixed vegetable soup is a traditional Thai dish. It focuses more on vegetables than meat and is healthy for everyone.
117 Kilocalorie

Recipe

  • 1+1/2 tsp black pepper
  • Fingerroot 1-2 stems
  • 3-4 shallots
  • Shrimp paste 1 teaspoon
  • 1/3 cup finely ground dried shrimp
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • Chinese okra, cut into bite-sized pieces 150 g
  • Pumpkin, cut into bite-sized pieces 200 g
  • Courgette, cut into bite size pieces 150 g
  • Baby corn, sliced 150 g
  • 150 g of champignon Mushrooms
  • 200 g of fresh shrimp
  • basil 80 g
  • fish sauce

Method

  1. Prepare the curry paste first. Pound the pepper with the Krachai, then add the shallots, pound thoroughly, followed by the shrimp paste, pound together, then add the ground dried shrimp, pound together again until thoroughly.
  2. Put the chicken broth in the pot. Bring to a boil and add curry paste.
  3. When the soup is boiling, add vegetables that are difficult to cook first. Bring to a boil again then add the rest of the vegetables. Once the water is boiling add the chinese okra, pumpkin, courgette and mushrooms.
  4. When the soup is boiling again, add fresh shrimp. Season with fish sauce as desired.
  5. When the mixture boils again, add the basil leaves. scoop served hot.
